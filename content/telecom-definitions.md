# Telecom Definitions (ATOM Canonical Glossary)

This document captures standard terms as used by SolveForce and ATOM.

- **Carrier** – A telecommunications provider supplying connectivity services.
- **Last-mile** – The final leg of the network from provider edge to customer premise.
- **Backbone** – High-capacity long-haul network segments interconnecting regions.
- **Edge device** – Customer or field-side device that terminates or consumes network service.
